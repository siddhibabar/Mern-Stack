About Ratan Tata

Ratan Naval Tata is one of India’s most respected and admired business leaders. He was born on 28 December 1937 and served as the Chairman of the Tata Group from 1991 to 2012.

Under his leadership, the Tata Group expanded globally and became a symbol of trust, ethics, and excellence.


Major Achievements

  • Led Tata Group to global recognition
  • Acquisition of Jaguar Land Rover and Corus Steel
  • Launched Tata Nano, the world’s most affordable car
  • Chairman of Tata Group for over two decades
  • Recipient of Padma Bhushan and Padma Vibhushan

Leadership and Values

Ratan Tata is admired not only for his business acumen but also for his humility, integrity, and compassion.

He strongly believes that businesses should serve society and contribute to nation-building.


Why Ratan Tata Is a Role Model

Ratan Tata inspires millions through his simple lifestyle, ethical leadership, and commitment to social welfare.

His journey teaches us that success and values can go hand in hand.
